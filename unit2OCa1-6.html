1.6 Describing access issues that need taking into account - describe issues that people with disabilities may face when using webpages and how we can overcome them

  • Common disabilities include:
  • 1. physical deformities that affect the upper and lower limbs
  • 2.manual dexterity problems
  • 3. inability to coordinate body organs and others.
  • Widening participation and disability legislation
  • Facilitating access through the use of assistive technology
  • technology that facilitates access to a standard PC,
  • technology that facilitates access to the Internet,
  • technology that facilitates access to and manipulation of written word
  • technology that facilitates access to and manipulation of spoken word,
  • technology that helps to compensate for cognitive deficits

    • Facilitating inclusivity through Universal Design

  • are developed with consideration of the needs of a diverse population,
  • are not described as being anything more than easy for everyone to use, Section one Introduction
